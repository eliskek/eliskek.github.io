Elis Knight

I am a filmmaker, composer and sound artist working across experimental and non-fiction forms. I am concerned with consciousness, labour, ecology, and the political possibilities of small-scale cinema.

In my work I like to prioritise D.I.Y. production, analogue process, and slow, methodical observation.

Alongside practical filmmaking, I have written essays on sustainability, radical praxis, and the relationships between consciousness and images.
